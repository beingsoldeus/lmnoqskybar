About Us

Perched high above the vibrant cityscape of Kolkata, LMNOQ Skybar offers an unparalleled rooftop experience that tantalizes all senses. Our sophisticated ambiance combines modern elegance with breathtaking panoramic views, creating the perfect backdrop for unforgettable evenings.

Indulge in our expertly crafted cocktails, curated by master mixologists, and savor gourmet dishes that fuse global flavors with local ingredients. As night falls, our venue transforms with live music performances and DJ sets that energize the atmosphere, making LMNOQ the ultimate destination for those seeking elevated social experiences.

Featured in Whatshot Kolkata, we've quickly become the city's most talked-about social hotspot, where connections are forged and celebrations come alive under the stars.
